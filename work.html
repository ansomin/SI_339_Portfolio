Works and Projects

Here are some of the works I have done during my college career.

Version Control System for Virtual Reality

I worked with three other graduate students to design a version control system for virtual reality specifically for interior design. Went through the process of sketching, designing, and presenting storyboards to potential users for feedback. Worked with Unity to implement the actual Virtual Reality environment. Used projects, such as Mixed Reality Toolkit by Microsoft, to implement motions to manipulate objects.

UnityPhilly Data Analysis

The success of overdose prevention programs is contingent upon the willingness and effectiveness of bystanders to respond to an overdose event and administer naloxone. Using the UnityPhilly app, members can signal that someone is overdosing and others with the app can respond with naloxone. I analyzed data by sorting and extracting relevant data from a case study on emergency community response to opioid overdose in Philadelphia.
